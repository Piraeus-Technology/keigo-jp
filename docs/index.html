Privacy Policy

KeiGo! — Japanese Keigo & Politeness Levels · Last updated: March 2026

KeiGo! does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

KeiGo! (com.lkh9596.keigo) is a Japanese keigo and politeness level reference app. It is designed to work entirely on your device. We believe in keeping things simple — including our privacy practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information. We do not collect location data. We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data. We do not use cookies or similar technologies. We do not collect device identifiers.

Local Storage

KeiGo! uses AsyncStorage to store your favorites, history, quiz statistics, and theme preference locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time within the app.

Text-to-Speech

KeiGo! uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to provide audio pronunciation. No audio data is sent to external servers — all speech synthesis happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

KeiGo! does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No account or sign-in is required to use the app.

Children's Privacy

KeiGo! does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Given the nature of the app, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at contact@piraeus.app.
